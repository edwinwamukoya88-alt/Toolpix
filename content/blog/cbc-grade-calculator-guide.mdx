## Definition: CBC Grade Calculator

A CBC Grade Calculator is a specialized tool designed for Kenyan teachers implementing the Competency-Based Curriculum. It automates the calculation of learner scores across multiple assessment areas and converts raw scores into the standard CBC competency levels:

- **EE (Exceeding Expectations)** — 80–100% — Learner consistently performs above the expected standard
- **ME (Meeting Expectations)** — 60–79% — Learner meets the expected standard consistently
- **AE (Approaching Expectations)** — 40–59% — Learner is making progress but not yet at standard
- **BE (Below Expectations)** — 0–39% — Learner requires significant support

## How It Works: Score Calculation

The calculator uses a weighted average formula to determine final scores:

- **Projects** (typically weighted 30%) — Entered as separate assessment entries
- **Written tests** (typically weighted 40%) — All scored assessments included
- **Practical tasks** (typically weighted 20%) — Observation and performance scores
- **Portfolio reviews** (typically weighted 10%) — Cumulative assessment records

Each component score is multiplied by its assigned weight, summed, and divided by the total weight to produce a final percentage. The percentage automatically determines the competency level based on KICD guidelines.
